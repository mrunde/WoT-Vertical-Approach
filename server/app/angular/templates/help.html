How can I create a User?

The steps needed are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Go to the Main-Page
  • Click Sign-Up in the top-right corner
  • Enter your Email address and Password (Write down your Password you can't change it afterwards
  • Click Signup and you are done

Enter your email adress and password and Login
