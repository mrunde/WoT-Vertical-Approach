Twitter Configuration

The following tutorial shows in brief steps how to setup the Posting of Twitter-Notifications (e.g. Flood-Warnings).
Hint: A Twitter Account is needed to publish Notifications. You can create one here.

  • Go to the Twitter-Application-Management area and create a new Application
    Click here to create a new Twitter-Application.


  • Enter all necessary informations in the folowing dialog


  • Now, after you successfully created an application you will receive access to Application keys and Access Tokens (avaiblibe under “Keys and Access Tokens”

    in the applications options.


  • Finally add the Keys and Tokens to your WoT-Acount:
    Login into your account and click the button “Edit Profile Settings”.

    In the following dialog you can enter your keys and tokens as shown in your Twitter Account.
