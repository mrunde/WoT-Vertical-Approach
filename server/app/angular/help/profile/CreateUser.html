How to create a Proifle and Login

Creating a profile is fairly easy. The following steps will guide you through the process:

  • Click on the "Sign Up" Button in the top-right corner

  • Enter your (valid) email adress and a password. Afterwards click "Login".
    Attention! You can not reset your password after the registration! We recommend to write it down somewhere!
